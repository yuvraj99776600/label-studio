These onboarding guides will help you get started with your role on the platform.

Label Studio Enterprise supports a collaborative workflow where team members work together to create high-quality training data. The platform is designed around two primary roles:

## For Annotators

If you're new to annotating tasks in Label Studio, start with the **[Annotator onboarding guide](onboarding_annotator)**. This guide covers:

* How to access and navigate Label Studio
* Finding and working on your assigned projects
* Step-by-step annotation workflows
* Reviewing your performance analytics
* Best practices for efficient and accurate annotation

Annotators are responsible for creating high-quality labels and annotations that train machine learning models. Your work is essential to building accurate, reliable AI systems.

## For Reviewers

If you're reviewing annotation tasks, check out the **[Reviewer onboarding guide](onboarding_reviewer)**. This guide includes:

* How to access the review interface
* Reviewing and evaluating annotation tasks
* Providing feedback to annotators
* Understanding review metrics and analytics
* Best practices for consistent, thorough reviews

Reviewers ensure quality and consistency across all annotations, serving as the quality assurance layer before tasks move to production.
